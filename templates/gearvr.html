Gear VR Instructions

Instructions for Samsung Gear VR: Open this page on your computer and not your Samsung mobile. The following method applies to owners of a Samsung Gear VR and allows for viewing of select 360 VR Panoramas of SAOTA's architecture.

Download Files: Please download one/both of the zip files linked below from a computer and copy the contents of the extracted folder to your Samsung device. Additionally, make sure to Download Oculus 360 Photos from the Oculus Store.
